Delete Your Stop Tracker Account

We're sorry to see you go! This page explains how to request deletion of your Stop Tracker account and all associated data.

⚠️ Important Warning

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Once your account is deleted, you will lose:

  • All delivery stop records and earnings data
  • All saved routes and route history
  • All generated invoices and client information
  • All statistics and analytics data
  • All app settings and preferences

We recommend exporting your data before requesting deletion.

How to Export Your Data Before Deleting

  1. Open the Stop Tracker app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Tap "Export Data to CSV"
  4. Save the exported file to your device
  5. Optionally, save any important invoices or routes

How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1: Delete Through the App (Fastest)

  1. Open the Stop Tracker app on your device
  2. Go to Settings (tap the gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to the Account section
  4. Tap "Delete All Data"
  5. Confirm your choice
  6. Your data will be permanently deleted immediately

Option 2: Request Deletion by Email

📧 Email Us at: davidwboni@gmail.com

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Subject Line: "Stop Tracker Account Deletion Request"
  • Email Address: The email address associated with your Stop Tracker account
  • User Name: Your name as registered in the app
  • Confirmation: State "I confirm I want to permanently delete my Stop Tracker account and all associated data"

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, we will permanently remove:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and login credentials
  • Delivery Data: All stop records, dates, earnings, and statistics
  • Routes: All saved routes and route history
  • Invoices: All client information, invoice history, and generated PDFs
  • Settings: All app preferences and customizations
  • Cloud Backups: All data stored in Firebase/cloud storage

Deletion Timeline

In-App Deletion: Immediate (instant deletion)

Email Request: Within 30 days of receiving your request

You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • You will no longer be able to log in to your account
  • All your data will be permanently removed from our servers
  • You can create a new account anytime if you wish to use Stop Tracker again
  • Previous data cannot be recovered after deletion

Alternative: Keep Your Account But Clear Data

If you want to keep using Stop Tracker but start fresh:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Use "Clear Data" instead of "Delete Account"
  3. This removes your records but keeps your account active
