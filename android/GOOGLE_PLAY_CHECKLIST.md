## 3. Privacy Policy (REQUIRED!)

Since your app uses geolocation, you MUST provide a privacy policy URL.

**Quick Options**:
1. Use a privacy policy generator:
   - https://www.privacypolicygenerator.info/
   - https://app-privacy-policy-generator.firebaseapp.com/

2. Host it on:
   - Your own website
   - GitHub Pages (free)
   - Google Sites (free)

**What to Include**:
- What data you collect (location, delivery logs, user info)
- Why you collect it (route planning, analytics)
- How you store it (Firebase, local storage)
- Who has access (only the user)
- How users can delete their data
- Contact information
